James Clear

Atomic Habits

A routine or practice performed regularly; an automatic response to a specific situation.

James Clear is a leading expert in the field of habit formation and behavior change. He has spent years researching and writing about the science of habits, drawing on insights from psychology, neuroscience, and behavioral economics. In his work, Clear emphasizes the importance of small, consistent actions in creating lasting change, and provides practical strategies for overcoming common obstacles to behavior change.

Clear is also an accomplished entrepreneur, having founded a successful travel photography business and a popular personal development website. He is a sought-after speaker and has given talks at Fortune 500 companies, universities, and organizations around the world. Through his work, Clear has helped countless people improve their lives by developing better habits, increasing productivity, and achieving their goals.
